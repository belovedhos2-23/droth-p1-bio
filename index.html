About Me

Hello! My name is Danielle Roth. I'm a student in the Bachelor of Fine Arts in the Visual Communications Design program at Eastern Washington University. I am a native resident of Spokane, WA and live in Cheney, WA with my amazing husband, David. I love to read, spend time with family & friends, backpack, go horseback riding, and especially, to create. I enjoy many mediums of creation--from crocheting to logo desing, from woodburning to typography art.
